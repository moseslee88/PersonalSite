Hi, Welcome to Moses Lee's page

I came to Skill Distillery because I wanted to create something out of nothing. It was also a choice that came down to location, for being here creates a peace and eliminates many distractions that I may have back at home. Choosing to come here was a decision I made, based on multiple factors, such as the content curriculum, online reviews, and the acceptance of GI Bill.

Reasons for wanting to be a programmer were twofold, to become in sync with the current era and to become more resourceful. With out current generation of self-driving cars and emerging biotechnology and our plugged-in society, I have a desire to be able to join part of the programming crowd and use data and info to create different web applications. In addition, in Java, knowing you have the resources (and libraries) to find a solution makes daunting problems less scary. You might not know immediately how to solve a problem, but if you know where to look for help, you will be much more confident in tackling problems

We each have a story to tell; mine is just as unique as yours. I served 5 years in the U.S. Army, and I did my time as a cryptologic linguist. I learned that unforeseen things can not be predicted, but that certain things can be mitigated, with proper planning and preparation. I also learned that patterns of data can reveal interesting details, and that it is very important to be vigilant. We always need to be monitoring foreign communications/activity at all times; we need to be alert for the next potential act of terrorism or extremism.


After I graduate from Skill Distillery, I hope to land a job in sports analytics in the greater Los Angeles area.

I am an eager beaver employer I am currently in the booming field of software development I am merely looking to be pals with Moses!
